Digital strategy and EA roadmap after digital readiness assessment for a large manufacturer

  • Business challenge – This large manufacturer’s business model requires only dealing with dealers and distributors so they needed to undertake digital transformation to achieve end customer centricity and closer engagement, market penetration and beat the completion.

  • Solution – Identify digital initiatives, assess the IT landscape for required digital readiness of architecture with recommendations and EA roadmap prepared with business case for transformation.

  • Business benefits/outcomes – Swift decision making and digital strategy chalked out in record time with digital initiatives taking to the floor with business sponsorship and all readiness.

EA assessment and strategy alignment for a large insurance company

  • Business challenge – Organic growth and lack of governance in Finance and risk information delivery (FRID) needs EA assessment and 2020 strategy alignment

  • Solution – Holistic as-is assessment with recommendations and EA roadmap prepared for FRID system with business case for transformation.

  • Business benefits/outcomes – 50%+ Saving potential identified in OPEX, Resource bandwidth, productivity and increased business agility

Business capability review, IT architecture assessment and EA roadmap for energy company

  • Business challenge – Form the IT strategy to address a) Business growth and vision b) increase/improve business capability c) Propose a new EA roadmap.

  • Solution – Business, Operations and IT capability reviews, business case for transformation and vendor evaluation for capability improvement program

  • Business benefits/outcomes – New IT programs kicked off to increase capability.

EA repository and governance setup for health insurance & wellness products company

  • Business challenge – No central EA repository and roadmap for wellness products and lack of governance with a consolidated view

  • Solution – Evaluate and select EA modelling tool, build central EA repository and establish governance. Document AS-IS architecture

  • Business benefits/outcomes – Enabled strategic decision making, improved productivity and ROI on IT initiatives due to governance
